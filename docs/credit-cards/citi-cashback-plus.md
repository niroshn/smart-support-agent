# Citi Cash Back+ Card

## Overview
The Citi Cash Back+ Card offers one of the highest flat-rate cashback in Singapore - no categories to track, no caps to worry about. Perfect for those who want simplicity.

## Key Benefits
- **1.6% unlimited cashback** on all spending - no category restrictions
- No minimum spend requirement
- **No annual fee** forever (apply through SingSaver)
- Worldwide acceptance with Visa network
- Complimentary travel insurance when you charge flights to the card

## Eligibility Requirements
- Minimum annual income: $30,000
- Singapore citizen or Permanent Resident
- Age 21-65 years old
- Clean credit record with no bankruptcy history

## Required Documents
- NRIC (front and back)
- Latest 12 months CPF contribution history, OR
- Latest 3 months computerised payslips, OR
- Latest Notice of Assessment (for self-employed)

## Fees and Charges
| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Annual Fee | $192.60 (waived forever via SingSaver) |
| Late Payment Fee | $100 |
| Finance Charge | 28% p.a. |
| Cash Advance Fee | 6% (min $15) |
| Foreign Transaction Fee | 3.25% |
| Balance Transfer Fee | One-time 1.5-2.5% |

## Why Choose This Card?
- **Best for**: People who hate tracking spending categories
- **Unique selling point**: Truly unlimited cashback with no caps
- **Pro tip**: Great as a "catch-all" card for miscellaneous spending

## Cashback Calculation Example
Monthly spend of $2,000:
- Cashback earned: $2,000 x 1.6% = $32
- Annual cashback: $384
- No annual fee = pure savings

## How to Apply
1. Click "Apply Now" on SingSaver
2. Complete online application (10 minutes)
3. Submit documents via secure upload
4. Receive approval within 2-3 business days
5. Activate card upon receipt
