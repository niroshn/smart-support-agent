# HSBC Revolution Card

## Overview
The HSBC Revolution Card is designed for digital spenders, offering exceptional rewards on online shopping, entertainment, and dining. One of the best no-fee cards for millennials and Gen Z.

## Key Benefits
- **4 miles per $1** (or 2.5% cashback) on online spend, dining, and entertainment
- **0.4 miles per $1** on all other spending
- **No annual fee** forever
- No minimum spend requirement
- Flexible redemption: miles OR cashback

## Bonus Categories (4X Rewards)
- Online shopping (Shopee, Lazada, Amazon, etc.)
- Streaming (Netflix, Spotify, Disney+, YouTube Premium)
- Food delivery (GrabFood, Deliveroo, foodpanda)
- Restaurants and dining
- Entertainment (movies, concerts, events)
- Taxi/ride-hailing (Grab, Gojek, ComfortDelGro)

## Eligibility Requirements
- Minimum annual income: $30,000
- Singapore citizen, PR, or Employment Pass holder
- Age 21-65 years old

## Required Documents
- NRIC or Employment Pass
- Latest 3 months payslips, OR
- Latest 12 months CPF contribution history, OR
- Latest 2 years Notice of Assessment (self-employed)

## Fees and Charges

## Rewards Calculation

### Example: $2,000 monthly spend
If 60% is on bonus categories:
- Bonus spend: $1,200 x 4 miles = 4,800 miles
- Other spend: $800 x 0.4 miles = 320 miles
- **Total: 5,120 miles/month = 61,440 miles/year**

### Cashback Option
- Convert rewards to cashback at 1% rate for bonus categories
- Useful if you don't travel frequently
