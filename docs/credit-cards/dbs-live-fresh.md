# DBS Live Fresh Card

## Overview
The DBS Live Fresh Card is designed for young professionals and digital natives who want to earn cashback on online spending and contactless payments.

## Key Benefits
- **5% cashback** on online spending and contactless transactions (capped at $20/month)
- **1% cashback** on all other spending
- **No annual fee** for the first 2 years
- Exclusive deals on food delivery and streaming services
- Mobile wallet compatible (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay)

## Eligibility Requirements
- Minimum annual income: $30,000
- Singapore citizen, PR, or Employment Pass holder
- Age 21-65 years old
- Good credit history

## Required Documents
- NRIC (front and back) or Employment Pass
- Latest computerised payslip OR latest 3 months CPF contribution history
- Proof of residence (if different from NRIC address)

## Fees and Charges
| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Annual Fee | $194.40 (waived first 2 years) |
| Late Payment Fee | $100 |
| Cash Advance Fee | 6% of amount (min $15) |
| Foreign Currency Transaction | 3.25% |
| Overlimit Fee | $40 |
| Card Replacement | $30 |

## How to Apply
1. Visit SingSaver.com.sg or the DBS website
2. Fill in the online application form
3. Upload required documents
4. Receive in-principle approval within minutes
5. Card delivered within 5-7 business days

## Rewards Program
- Cashback credited automatically to your statement
- No minimum spend required for base 1% cashback
- Online and contactless bonus requires minimum $600 spend/month
- Cashback does not expire
