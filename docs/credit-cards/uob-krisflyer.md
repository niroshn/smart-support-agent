# UOB KrisFlyer Credit Card

## Overview
The UOB KrisFlyer UOB Card is the go-to card for Singapore Airlines frequent flyers. Earn KrisFlyer miles on every purchase and enjoy accelerated earning on travel and dining.

## Key Benefits
- **3 miles per $1** on overseas spend and online travel bookings
- **2 miles per $1** on dining, food delivery, and Grab rides
- **1.4 miles per $1** on all other spending
- Miles credited directly to your KrisFlyer account
- Complimentary travel insurance up to $1,000,000
- Priority Pass lounge access (2 free visits/year)

## Eligibility Requirements
- Minimum annual income: $50,000
- Singapore citizen or Permanent Resident
- Age 21-65 years old
- Existing KrisFlyer membership (free to sign up)

## Required Documents
- NRIC (Singapore citizens/PRs)
- Latest 3 months payslips or latest NOA
- Proof of KrisFlyer membership number

## Miles Earning Structure

### Accelerated Categories (3 mpd)
- Hotels booked online (Agoda, Booking.com, etc.)
- Flights (any airline)
- Overseas retail spending
- Cruise bookings

### Bonus Categories (2 mpd)
- Restaurants and cafes
- Food delivery (GrabFood, Deliveroo, foodpanda)
- Grab rides
- Streaming services

### Base Earning (1.4 mpd)
- Groceries
- Utilities
- Insurance
- All other purchases

2. Apply via SingSaver for exclusive welcome bonus
3. Submit income documents
