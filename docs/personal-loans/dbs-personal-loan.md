# DBS Personal Loan

## Overview
DBS Personal Loan offers competitive rates and flexible tenures for Singaporeans looking to consolidate debt, fund home renovations, or cover major expenses. As Singapore's largest bank, DBS provides reliability and convenience.

## Key Features
- Loan amount: **$3,000 to $250,000**
- Interest rate: **3.5% to 9% p.a.** (EIR 6.7% to 17% p.a.)
- Tenure: **1 to 7 years**
- No collateral required (unsecured loan)
- Same-day approval for existing DBS customers
- Funds disbursed within 1 business day upon approval

## Interest Rate Tiers
| Annual Income | Interest Rate (Flat) | EIR |
|---------------|---------------------|-----|
| $120,000+ | From 3.5% p.a. | 6.7% p.a. |
| $80,000-$119,999 | From 4.5% p.a. | 8.6% p.a. |
| $50,000-$79,999 | From 5.5% p.a. | 10.4% p.a. |
| $30,000-$49,999 | From 7% p.a. | 13.2% p.a. |

*Rates subject to credit assessment. EIR = Effective Interest Rate*

## Eligibility Requirements
- Minimum annual income: $30,000
- Singapore citizen or Permanent Resident
- Age 21-65 years old
- Employed for at least 6 months
- No history of bankruptcy

## Required Documents
- NRIC (front and back)
- Latest 3 months payslips, OR
- Latest 12 months CPF contribution history, OR
- Latest 2 years Notice of Assessment

## Fees and Charges
| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Processing Fee | 1-3% of loan amount |
| Early Repayment Fee | 3% of outstanding balance |
| Late Payment Fee | $100 + 3% of overdue amount |
| Returned Payment | $30 |

## How to Apply
1. Check your eligibility on SingSaver
2. Compare rates across lenders
3. Apply online (10 minutes)
4. Submit documents via secure upload
5. Receive approval within 24 hours
6. Sign loan agreement digitally
7. Funds credited to your account

## What Can You Use It For?
- Debt consolidation (credit card, other loans)
- Home renovation
- Wedding expenses
- Medical bills
- Education fees
- Major purchases
- Emergency funds

## Tips for Getting Better Rates
1. Maintain a good credit score (check via Credit Bureau Singapore)
2. Apply with income proof showing stability
3. Existing DBS customers often get preferential rates
4. Consider shorter tenure for lower total interest
5. Compare rates on SingSaver before applying
